Philosophy

No conversation needed.
Just being noticed is enough.

Social psychology research shows that even a minimal signal from an anonymous person can restore a sense of belonging. What matters is not what is said, but that someone recognized you.

The button you press to help yourself quietly becomes help for someone else. Connection by connection, we work toward a world with a little less loneliness.
